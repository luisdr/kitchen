My Kitchen Recipes

Apple Cake

Ingredients: Apples, flour, sugar, eggs.

Instructions: Mix ingredients. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes. Serve.

Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients: Milk, cream, vanilla extract, sugar.

Instructions: Mix ingredients. Freeze for 4 hours. Serve.

Nachos

Ingredients: Tortilla chips, cheese, salsa.

Instructions: Layer chips with cheese and salsa. Bake for 5 minutes. Serve warm.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients: Flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs.

Instructions: Mix ingredients. Bake at 180°C for 25 minutes. Serve.
